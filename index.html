About Shenzhou Qigong Tuina

Welcome to Shenzhou Qigong Tuina, a neighborhood space offering Chinese massage and wellness treatments. Located in New York City, we focus on helping you relax, reduce tension, and support overall wellbeing through attentive bodywork.

Our practitioners have experience with traditional techniques such as Tuina, acupressure, and Qigong-informed bodywork. Treatments are tailored to your comfort and needs, and our team will work with you to find the right approach for relief and relaxation.

We value the restorative benefits of therapeutic touch and the insights of traditional Chinese practices. Each session aims to improve circulation, ease muscle tension, and restore a sense of calm in a comfortable, soothing environment.

Visit us to experience caring, personalized bodywork. Your comfort and wellbeing are our priorities.
